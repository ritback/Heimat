Foster & Goldtein

This is some album art-work I made for a band: Foster & Goldstein.
The background was made with a procedural texture calculated with OpenFrameworks. It's a manipulation of Perlin's noise texture. The colors are added using Perlin's different noises. I used a GUI add-on of OpenFramework to create a software where it's possible to play with differents parameters. The software has an animation you can use to stop and export textures in HD.

When I obtain the textures, I retouch colors with Photoshop and sometimes vectorized the texture with Illustrator.

The font is Times New Roman! I love to play with classical fonts and make them my own. Point by point I retouch the font to obtain exactly what I want.

The band wanted "...a law office business card on acid".

Reg'art

The association Reg'art allows amateur artists to display their works throughout the urban space of Grenoble. It aims to develop the cultural identity of the city of Grenoble by posting art works where there would otherwise be advertisements--with the blessing of the city administration.

You can get more info on the web site I designed and coded for the association: regart.org.
