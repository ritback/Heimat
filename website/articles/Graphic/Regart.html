Reg'art

Graphic design, visual identity

About:

Visual identity and Web site developpement for the association reg'art.

The association Reg'art allows amateur artists to display their works throughout the urban space of Grenoble. It aims to develop the cultural identity of the city of Grenoble by posting art works where there would otherwise be advertisements--with the blessing of the city administration.

I designed and coded the web site for the association. Unfortunatly the wesite doesn't exist anymore...
