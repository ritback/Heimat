Work in Progress, wood wind rose

Art installation, carpentry, conception

About:

This is a work in progress.

A wind rose made of wood to symbolise the changes of direction in life. The petal should be moving parts, the flower opening and closing with certain interaction.

Petals would be cover with a very light paper so visuals can be projected on it.

I can only show 3D plans for now... more to come this month.
